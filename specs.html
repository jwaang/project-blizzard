Our Features

In the navigation bar, we have a universe setting which controls different aspects of the universe.

We decided to change a few aspects of the story to fit our project. We gave Izzy a kind of "shield" that, upon collision with a bolide, would protect Izzy and destory the bolide. There is also a counter above Izzy that shows how many Arklets are remaining.


The Arklets follow closely behind Izzy and if just a single bolide collides with an Arklet, both it and the bolide will be destroyed.
In addition, the counter will update how many arklets are left.


Once all the arklets have been destroyed (counter will display 0), every object will dissapear except a text displaying the amount of seconds Izzy and Arklets survived for.
