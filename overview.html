Project Overview

This project was developed for the Spring '16 CSCE 315 class at TAMU by Joseph Townsend, Jonathan Wang, Brandon Donohue and John Austin Moreno. It was written using HTML and Javascript and consists of a 3D simulation of the events that happen in the novel given above (with some slight adjustments).

Original Story

Earth's moon has exploded into 7 different large pieces and continue to orbit around it. Occasionally the pieces will collide with another and produce smaller fragments called bolides and begin to fall towards Earth. These bolides are no threat because they are small enough to be destroyed in the Earth's atmosphere, but over time, the collisions become more frequent and soon the Earth's sky is filled with these bolides. Then, the larger fragments begin to impact Earth and its surface is destoryed and its oceans are evaporated.

In attempts to save the population, Earth's nations send Arklets containing 1552 humans from the population into orbit. These Arklets stay close to the international space station, Izzy , which protects them from the bolids. The Parabulator is the management system used by Izzy that monitors incoming bolides and issues commands to the Arklets to avoid any collisions to protect the human race.
